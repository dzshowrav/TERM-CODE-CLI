# Synthesizing Results

Combine role outputs into one answer without losing truth.

## Process

1. List the outputs received.
2. Resolve conflicts explicitly.
3. Remove duplicate or contradicted claims.
4. Check the combined answer against the original goal.
5. Verify the combined answer has real evidence.

## Rule

Do not average conflicting claims into vague language. Choose the supported claim or mark the uncertainty.
